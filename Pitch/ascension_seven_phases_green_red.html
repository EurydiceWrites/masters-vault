ASCENSION

The seven ages

"You will never hit a ceiling, and you will never run out of world."

Part I — Base game
The Underworld

You are born in darkness. A slime in a cavern. A skeleton in a crypt. A hatchling in a volcanic fissure. You devour, you grow, you build your first domain. Other monsters are doing the same. Conquer them or be consumed.

Solo creature combat · Power acquisition · Domain seeding · Rival encounters · GenAI narrative identity

Part II — Base game
The Overworld

You breach the surface. One continent stretches before you, populated, defended, unprepared. Build armies from your minions. Command them in turn-based tactical combat. Drop into battle yourself when the opposing commander takes the field.

Army composition · Tactical combat · Commander drop-in · Diplomacy · Anti-doomstack warfare

Part III — Expansion
The Planet

This continent was only the beginning. Oceans, ice caps, deserts, and civilizations you haven't imagined yet. Some worlds fall easily. Others have been preparing for something like you for a very long time.

Naval & aerial forces · Climate warfare · Continental campaigns · Global logistics

Part IV — Expansion
The Solar System

The sky was never a ceiling. It was a door. Launch your first interplanetary campaign. Every world is different: some fractured and ripe for conquest, others unified under a single terrifying intelligence.

Interplanetary invasion · Footholds on hostile worlds · Diverse planetary biomes

Part V — Expansion
The Galaxy

Star systems. Billions of them. Your empire spans light-years. Each galaxy expansion is its own campaign: Alpha Centauri, Tau Ceti, the Magellanic Clouds. New physics, new creatures, new rules.

Galactic logistics · Star system campaigns · Expansion-specific factions · Cosmic-tier threats

Part VI — Expansion
The Universe

Every galaxy conquered. Every star claimed. The fabric of reality begins to feel thin. Something is on the other side. Something familiar.

Intergalactic warfare · Universal-scale diplomacy · Reality destabilization events

Part VII — Endgame
The Dimensions

You tear through the membrane of reality. On the other side: another universe. Another version of you. A creature that made every choice you didn't. The final conquest is yourself.

Reality-hopping · Alternate-self encounters · Dimensional physics · The mirror fight

YOUR EMPIRE CARRIES FORWARD. YOU NEVER START OVER.

Every expansion builds on everything you've built. The core loop never changes. The scale never stops growing.
