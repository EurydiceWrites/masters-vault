Build your domain
Spawning pools
Invest your power into the world. Create structures that defend, produce, and feed energy back to you. Every creature type builds differently. The Slime seeds pools. The Devil builds courts. The Dragon shapes lairs.
Your domain is not a base. It is an extension of your body.
Dual-layer combat
Command or conquer
Send your armies. Issue tactical orders turn by turn. When the opposing commander enters the fight, you get a choice: drop in. You are not a general watching from a tower. You are the most dangerous thing on the field.
No doomstacking. No cheating AI. What you scout is what you fight.

GenAI narrative engine
Your story. No one else's.
At every evolution milestone, the narrative engine reads your complete journey — every creature consumed, every rival defeated, every path chosen — and generates the identity of what you've become. Authored schema. Authored fallbacks. Your performance.
